I am a post-doc in the Prof. Bo Porse group at the University of Copenhagen, Denmark. I did my Ph.D. (bioinformatics) in 2013 under the supervision of Prof. Jan Gorodkin at the University of Copehnagen. My research interest is in applying statistical and computational methods for the analysis of high-throughput sequencing data in order to understand functional and regulatory aspect of the transcriptome. Specifically, I have developed computational algorithms to analyze high-throughput sequencing data for the prediction of non-coding RNAs [PMID: 22053076, 23874353, 24797614, 26042150] and to understand regulation in their post-transcriptional processing [26166713].

Prior to joining my Ph.D., I have gained research experience while working in various research laboratories in India. During this period, I developed computational algorithms based on machine learning methods like SVM for the identification of pathogenicity islands in prokaryotic genome [PMID: 19032158, 21904425].
