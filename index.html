I am a post-doctoral fellow in the laboratory of Prof. Bo Porse at the University of Copenhagen, Denmark. I work in the research area of computational biology. My current research is focused on to understand the role of two transcription factors (TF), PU.1 and CEBPA during differentiation of stem cells into mature blood cells (hematopoiesis). I use RNA-seq and ChIP-seq data to study how the expression of two TFs shapes the cis-regulatory landscape (enhancers) across different differentiation stages.

I did my Ph.D. (computational biology) in 2013 under the supervision of Prof. Jan Gorodkin at the University of Copenhagen. I developed and used computational and statistical methods to analyze RNA-seq data for the prediction of non-coding RNAs [PMID: 22053076, 23874353, 24797614, 26042150] and to understand regulation in their post-transcriptional processing [PMID: 26166713].
